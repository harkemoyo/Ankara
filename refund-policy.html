Refund Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Returns & Exchanges

We have a 14-day return policy, which means you have 14 days after receiving your item to request a return or exchange. Items must be unworn, in original packaging with tags intact.

2. How to Start a Return

To start a return, contact us at support@maryhumphrey.com with your order number. Returns sent back without prior authorization will not be accepted.
